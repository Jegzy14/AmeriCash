Timeline

2020

AmeriCash Loans Offers Same Day Funding

Your Money. Your Choice. We mean it! After listening to your requests, we are happy to introduce enhancements to our customer experience with Same Day Funding. Restrictions may apply.

2020

IMPROVED DIGITAL EXPERIENCE AT AMERICASHLOANS.NET

We've made improvements and launched a faster and much easier to use website so you can get the cash you need and get the questions you have answered in a more convenient matter.

2019

AMERICASH LOANS CONTINUES TO GROW

Our company continues to expand to new markets with new products and services to improve our customers experiences.

2018

AMERICASH LOANS WINS GRAPHIC DESIGN USA AWARD FOR BILLBOARD CREATION

Due to our latest rebranding and marketing processes we were granted with this award to recognize our efforts.

2017

AMERICASH LOANS CELEBRATES 20 YEAR ANNIVERSARY

AmeriCash Loans plans to celebrate our amazing journey to 20 years in business by thanking you, our loyal customers. Keep watching for some amazing news about our anniversary celebration in the coming months!

2016

RE-LAUNCH OF AMERICASHLOANS.NET!

You asked and we listened! We've overhauled and updated our website and Loan Management System in an effort to provide you with the greatest customer experience and get quick cash loans with a fast, easy, respectful and confidential experience.

2015

ALL STORE LOCATIONS GO ONLINE

Along with our efforts to make all of our online customers’ loan application process a great experience, we brought all of our locations into the online market, allowing our customers to decide when and where you want to find us.

2014

BRAND UPDATED AND RE-LAUNCHED

AmeriCash Loans brand is re-launched to match our customer’s expectations and meet the needs of an ever growing and evolving installment loan industry.

2014

OUR LOAN MANAGEMENT SYSTEM EXPANDS TO STORE LOCATIONS IN ILLINOIS, WISCONSIN AND MISSOURI

AmeriCash Loans launched and implemented our Loan Management System by formally introducing the process into our store locations in Illinois, Wisconsin, Missouri and South Carolina.

2013

AMERICASHLOANS.NET LAUNCHED!

Approval in three, two, one… We are live! AmeriCash Loans launches our new online loan website - www.americashloans.net - Licensed in Illinois, Wisconsin, and Missouri

2012

CREATION OF OUR PROPRIETARY LOAN MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Creation of Our Proprietary Loan Management System.

2010

WWW MEET AMERICASH LOANS – NOV. 1, 2010

AmeriCash Loans responded to customer requests to access loan applications online. Our awesome development team designed, developed and launched our first online loan platform - Checkbookloans.com - (no longer active today).

2010

INSTALLMENT LOAN ACT REVISION

AmeriCash Loans proactively works with regulators to revise the Consumer Installment Loan Act and Payday Loan Reform Act in Illinois in efforts to provide our customers with the service they need and deserve.

2008

EXPANDED INSTALLMENT LOANS IN ILLINOIS LOCATIONS

AmeriCash Loans refocused on providing the best product to help our customers by discontinuing offering cash advances in Illinois, to focus solely on expanding our installment loan product.

2007

EXPANDED LOCATIONS INTO SOUTH CAROLINA

Continued growth prompted AmeriCash Loans to open new locations in South Carolina; offering installment loans only.

2007

AMERICASH LOANS CELEBRATED 10 YEAR ANNIVERSARY!

Yay! We did it! (We couldn't have done it without your help, of course!) After 10 years in business, AmeriCash Loans had helped thousands of customers get quick cash loans with a fast, easy, respectful and confidential experience.

2005

EXPANDED LOCATIONS INTO MISSOURI

Further growth led AmeriCash Loans to open our locations in Missouri; offering installment loans and Checkbook® loans.

2004

EXPANDED LOCATIONS INTO WISCONSIN

AmeriCash growth and expansion led us to open locations in Wisconsin; offering both installment loans and cash advances to our customers.

2003

REFER-A-FRIEND PROGRAM LAUNCHED

Our customers are our greatest resource and to show our appreciation to you, we launched the Refer-A-Friend program. Check out the great benefits of this industry ground breaking program here, Refer-A-Friend FAQ, where we put cash back in your pocket and your friends and family, too!

2002

GREW TO 28 STORE LOCATIONS!

In just five years, as our loan process and loan product portfolio grew, so did our customers (that’s you!) and we successfully opened 28 stores in Illinois by the end of the year. Thank you!

2001

AMERICASH LOANS INTRODUCED INSTALLMENT LOANS IN ILLINOIS

AmeriCash Loans introduces installment loans; a short-term loan that allows borrowers to access larger loan amounts for longer terms and at lower rates.

1998

OPENED OUR FIRST STORE!

AmeriCash Loans opened our first store in Des Plaines, Illinois. We began making short-term loans available to our customers by offering cash advances in times of need.

1997

AMERICASH LOANS, LLC FOUNDED

AmeriCash Loans is founded in 1997 in Des Plaines, Illinois in a small office above what would be our first store location.
