Our Story

  • Our Story

AmeriCash Loans was founded in 1997 in Des Plaines, Illinois on the main street across from the train station in a small office above what would become our first cash loan store location.

Within five years of opening our doors, we had expanded into 28 total locations throughout the metropolitan Chicago area and Illinois. With such incredible growth we wanted to share our success with our customers and launched our Refer-A-Friend program giving you a chance to earn while sharing your great experience being a customer of AmeriCash Loans with your friends and family.

We saw continued success, thanks to our great customers, allowing us to expand our store locations into Wisconsin first in 2004, closely followed by Missouri in 2005, South Carolina in 2007.

20 years later we have opened a total of 63 brick-and-mortar locations across the Illinois, Wisconsin, Missouri, and South Carolina areas. 

Our growth and success could not have been possible without each of our customers. We value your business and that’s why giving back to the community is very important to us. Our involvement ranges from sponsoring local youth programs, donating and partnering with local organizations, to hosting community events. 

AmeriCash Loans is here to build lasting relationships with each of our customers. We are committed to providing a superior customer service experience whether in-store or online. Our goal is to ensure that each experience is a memorable, respectful one that addresses each individual’s specific financial needs in a convenient matter.


Your Money. Your Choice. 

AmeriCash Loans celebrated our 20 year anniversary and continues to focus on providing the best products to help our customers by discontinuing offering cash advances and instead focused on expanding our installment loan product.
