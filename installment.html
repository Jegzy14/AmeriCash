Installment Loans

AmeriCash Loans offers installment loans with our online application, convienent locations and quick funding process. Find out everything you need to know about our financial product before applying.


What Is An Installment Loan?

An installment loan is a short-term loan that is paid back over a series of payments. For your convenience, at AmeriCash Loans we offer loans online and in-store.
Apply online, provide the required information, and in minutes you ll find out your approval status. Then, log into your customer portal to sign your documents.
Recieve your funds through direct deposit as fast as the same day if you apply before 11:30a CST or in as little as twenty-four hours. Restrictions may apply.

What should I have ready before applying?


Valid Driver's License or State ID
Verified Bank Account
Current Proof of Income

What can I use my installment loan for?


Medical Bills
Car Repairs
Rent Payments
Financial Emergencies
Anything!

How much can I borrow?


Loan amounts are regulated by the state in which you are applying from. When you apply, you will only be able to request money up to that state mandated amount.
